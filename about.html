About Us

Cipher Asset Recovery was founded by Cyber Intelligence, Crypto Investigations, Asset Recovery and Offshore Legal Experts. With over 20 years of experience in these fields individually they came together to form a boutique Cyber and Crypto Intelligence Group focused on providing results.

Our advanced technology and expertise can help law enforcement agencies, cryptocurrency exchanges and banks by accelerating their own investigations and reducing overall processing time
